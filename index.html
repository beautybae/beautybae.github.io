Microneedling Therapy

Collagen induction therapy, also known as microneedling is used to stimulate collagen and therefore soften textural components of the skin including fine lines, pore size, and scarring. We use a medical device for our microneedling procedures. This automated microneedling device causes focused and purposefully micro-trauma to the skin, which leads to the production of collagen and elastin. The benefits of Collagen Induction Therapy include softening of the appearance of scars and stretch marks, in addition to reduction of textural issues such as fine lines and wrinkles. This treatment can also improve the appearance of pores and gives a healthy, natural glow to the skin.

Hair Rejuvenation

Hair loss is an uncomfortable condition that many people suffer from. At Danesthetics Medical Design, we offer PRP injections to the scalp as a solution specifically for hair restoration. When PRP is injected into the scalp, it stimulates growth factors in the cells to encourage the scalp to grow hair again, producing thicker hair with time. These quick injections are more affordable than more invasive methods of hair restoration and can be done on your lunch break.
